Tan Yue (岳潭)
Email: yeutan [AT] pku [DOT] edu [DOT] cn

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University, and work with Prof. Dongyan Zhao. I received my PhD through the joint program between the Nanyang Technological University and Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, advised by Prof. Zonghai Hu and Prof. Erik Cambria.

  Research Interests

My research interests include multimodal information processing (ICLR 2023), natural language processing (COLING 2024), and multimodal fusion (Information Fusion 2023). Now my research focuses on multimodal large model perception and reasoning.
